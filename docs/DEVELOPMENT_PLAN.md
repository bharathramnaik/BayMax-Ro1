# BayMax-Ro1 Development Plan

> Comprehensive roadmap for building the AI-powered diagnostic system.

---

## Executive Summary

**Mission:** Build a portable, affordable AI diagnostic device for underserved populations.

**Vision:** A BayMax-inspired spectacle device that performs non-invasive health screening and provides AI-assisted diagnosis.

**Timeline:** 18 months to production-ready prototype

**Budget Estimate:** $150,000 for MVP (Phase 1-2)

## Risk Management

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Sensor accuracy | Medium | High | Multi-sensor fusion, calibration |
| LLM hallucination | High | Critical | Confidence thresholds, human review |
| Hardware failure | Low | Medium | Redundancy, rugged design |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Regulatory rejection | Medium | High | Early engagement, phased approach |
| Funding gaps | Medium | High | Multiple funding sources |
| Team turnover | Low | High | Equity, mission-driven culture |

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Technical:** Extensive testing, fallback systems
2. **Regulatory:** Engage consultants early, pilot programs
3. **Business:** Diversified funding, lean operations
